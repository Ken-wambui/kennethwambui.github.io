PCB Design Engineer

🎓
BEng (Hons) Mechatronics Engineering Dedan Kimathi University of Technology — Nyeri, Kenya

Specialized PCB Design Engineer and Mechatronics Engineer with a proven record designing high-speed, impedance-controlled, multi-layer PCBs for UAV, IoT, industrial, and consumer applications.

Proficient in Altium Designer and KiCad across the full hardware development lifecycle — from schematic capture through DFM-ready Gerber output. Deep practical expertise in signal integrity, power integrity, EMI/EMC mitigation, mixed-signal design, and high-speed interfaces (HDMI, USB 3.2, CAN-FD, CSI).

With experience at Ailwing Drones Limited and AfrifabriLabs / ZeroBionics, I've led end-to-end hardware design on flight controllers, agricultural IoT, and industrial systems. Equally capable in embedded firmware (ESP32 / STM32, FreeRTOS) to validate and bring up custom hardware.
